New 2026

RL Post-Trained Medical Model with German University Hospital

We applied reinforcement learning post-training to a Qwen2.5-7B model on medical decision-making tasks in collaboration with a large German university hospital. The RL-trained model achieved a +30% improvement over o4-mini on clinical benchmarks - demonstrating that small, specialised models can outperform much larger general-purpose models when trained with the right methodology.
