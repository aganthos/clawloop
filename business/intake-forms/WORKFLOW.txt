AGANTHOS — INTAKE FORM WORKFLOW
================================

FIND a relevant corporate partnership / intake form online
    |
    v
SAVE the form questions into:
    intake forms_instructions/<OrgName>
    (paste URL on line 1, then all questions below)
    |
    v
GENERATE a draft by running:
    python main.py answer --form "<OrgName>"
    |
    v
DRAFT is saved automatically to:
    intake forms_claude suggestion/answers_<orgname>.md
    (Claude-generated — do not submit directly)
    |
    v
REVIEW & ADAPT the draft — copy to:
    intake form_adapted answers for uploading/<OrgName>
    edit for tone, accuracy, and personal context
    |
    v
SUBMIT — paste adapted answers into the live form
    and upload pitch deck PDF
